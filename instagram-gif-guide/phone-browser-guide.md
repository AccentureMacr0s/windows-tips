# How To: Add a GIF to Instagram Using a Phone Browser (No App Required)

This guide covers the complete workflow entirely from a mobile browser — no desktop, no Instagram app, and no Giphy app needed.

---

## Requirements

- A smartphone with a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari)
- A [Giphy](https://giphy.com) account
- An [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com) account

---

## Part 1 — Create a GIF on Your Phone

### Option A: Convert an Existing Video to GIF (browser-based)

1. Open your phone's browser and go to [ezgif.com/video-to-gif](https://ezgif.com/video-to-gif).
2. Tap **Choose file** → select a video from your phone's gallery (`.mp4`, `.mov`).
3. Set:
   - **Start time** / **End time** — trim to 2–6 seconds.
   - **Size** — set width to `480` pixels.
   - **Frame rate** — `15` fps.
4. Tap **Convert to GIF**.
5. Scroll down to the result and tap **Save** (or long-press the GIF → **Download image**).

### Option B: Record Directly and Convert

1. Record a short video (2–6 sec) with your phone camera.
2. Follow Option A to convert it.

### Option C: Use an AI Tool from the Browser

1. Open [pika.art](https://pika.art) or [runwayml.com](https://runwayml.com) in your phone browser.
2. Generate a short AI video clip.
3. Download the result.
4. Convert to GIF via Ezgif (see Option A above).

> **Tip:** Tap the browser's **desktop site** toggle if a site doesn't load properly on mobile.

---

## Part 2 — Upload the GIF to Giphy from Your Phone Browser

1. Open [giphy.com](https://giphy.com) in your phone browser.
2. If prompted, tap **Request Desktop Site** (in the browser menu) — the mobile version of Giphy sometimes hides the Upload button.
3. Sign in to your Giphy account.
4. Tap the **Upload** button (top-right).
5. Tap **Choose File** → select your downloaded GIF.
6. Fill in:
   - **Source URL** — your Instagram profile link (e.g. `https://www.instagram.com/yourhandle`).
   - **Tags** — add your name, brand, and niche keywords separated by commas. Example:
     ```
     юрий клинский, yuriy klinsky, klinsky, motivation, fitness
     ```
7. Tap **Upload to GIPHY**.
8. Copy the GIF URL from the share page — you will need it for validation (see `validate-gif.ps1`).

---

## Part 3 — Search for Your GIF in Instagram from a Phone Browser

Instagram's GIF sticker search is only available inside the app. However, you can **verify** that your GIF is live and tagged correctly directly from the Giphy website:

1. Open [giphy.com](https://giphy.com) in your phone browser.
2. In the search bar, type one of your tags (e.g. `юрий клинский`).
3. Confirm your GIF appears in results.

Once confirmed, it will also appear in Instagram's GIF sticker search within 24–48 hours of your Giphy channel being verified.

---

## Part 4 — Use Your GIF in Instagram (Phone Browser)

Instagram Stories and Reels **require the app** for the GIF sticker. However, you can share your Giphy GIF in a few ways from the browser:

### Share via Instagram DMs (browser)

1. Open [instagram.com](https://www.instagram.com) in your phone browser and enable **Desktop Site**.
2. Open a conversation.
3. Copy your GIF's direct Giphy URL (e.g. `https://media.giphy.com/media/XXXX/giphy.gif`).
4. Paste the URL into the message and send — it will embed as a GIF preview in most browsers.

### Share as a Post

1. Convert the GIF back to `.mp4` using [ezgif.com/gif-to-mp4](https://ezgif.com/gif-to-mp4).
2. Upload the `.mp4` to Instagram as a **Reel** or **Post** from the browser (enable Desktop Site).

## Tips for Phone-Only Workflow

| Situation                           | Solution                                                    |
|-------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
| Upload button missing on Giphy      | Enable **Request Desktop Site** in browser menu             |
| GIF file too large to upload        | Use Ezgif **Optimize** tool to reduce file size below 15 MB |
| Can't find GIF sticker in Instagram | GIF stickers require the Instagram app; browser DM workaround available (see Part 4) |
| Giphy account requires email verify | Check spam folder; use a simple email without special characters |
